Connect a bot

Paste the token BotFather gave you, then press Turn bot ON. The bot runs live — no restart needed.

How to get a token (30 seconds, one time)

  1. Open Telegram and message @BotFather.
  2. Send /newbot and follow the prompts (name + username).
  3. Copy the token it gives you (looks like 123456789:AA...).
  4. Paste it above and press Turn bot ON.

Your workers never touch BotFather — they just open the bot and tap /route. Add them under Workers using their Telegram ID.
